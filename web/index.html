Intro.

My name is Ephrem Tadesse Degu. I’m Graduate of information technology with a specialization in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing from Jimma University in 2017. I have gained distinctive academic achievements and hands-on research experience. I have over 4 years of research and teaching experience at Jimma university, faculty of computing and Informatics. I have been working a research on Natural Language Processing(NLP) and Information extraction tasks. One of the prominent information extraction task; which I have conducted so far is “event and temporal information extraction from unstructured Amharic text”. I would say that I am strongly familiar with knowledge engineering approaches and machine learning algorithms.
